Covent Garden


Covent Garden's market buildings and piazza are decorated by 40 mistletoe chandeliers and a towering, 55 ft Christmas tree. In Covent Garden you can find shops, stalls and can catch street performers and musicians in one of the open air cobbled piazzas. When you're done shopping, you can grab a bite to eat in one of the many restaurants that surround the marketplace.


Address: Covent Garden, WC2E 8RD     Website: coventgarden.london

Oxford Street


You know the festive season is underway when London's much loved Oxford Street is transformed by over 750,000 LED lightbulbs. As well as Christmas lights, Oxford Street is host to the high street's most well loved stores, making it the perfect place to do some last minute gift shopping.

Address: Oxford Circus, W1C 1LS     Website: oxfordstreet.co.uk
