Coppa Club Igloos


For Christmas, the terrace at Coppa Club is transformed into a winter dining experience. Instead of booking a table, you can hire an igloo and dine whilst enjoying unrivalled views of London's Thameside skyline. As well as winter dining classics, for 2018 Coppa Club have collaborated with Sipsmith gins to bring you a festive cocktail menu.


Address: 3 Three Quays Walk, EC3R 6AH     Website: coppaclub.co.uk

Dalloway Terrace


Dalloway Terrace is an outdoor, terraced restaurant in the heart of Bloomsbury. The terrace has been transformed into a winter hideaway. With the decor arranged by master florist Nikki Tibbles, Dalloway Terrace is a unique place to grab brunch or have afternoon tea over the festive period.


Address: Great Russell Street, WC1B 3NN     Website: dallowayterrace.com

Roof East


The Roof East popup venue is back with an 80's throwback alpine sports theme. You'll be able to try your hand at curling at Sliders; the 6 lane, rooftop curling experience. Lane side hosts can serve you cocktails or mulled wine from the neon-hit Ski Lodge Bar keep the competitive spirit going.

Address: Great Eastern Way, E15 1BB     Website: roofeast.com
